Empire of the Sun is a strategic card-driven wargame covering the entire War in the Pacific, from the attack on Pearl Harbor to Japan's surrender. Players take the roles of MacArthur, Yamamoto, Nimitz, and Mountbatten, directing forces across a vast theater stretching from India to Hawaii and from Alaska to Australia. The game blends the tension of a card-driven system with classic hex wargame mechanics, featuring a two-tiered combat system that resolves air-naval combat before ground operations. The key strategic variable is U.S. political will — Japan wins by forcing a negotiated peace through eliminating Allied nations and inflicting massive casualties. Yearly scenarios (1942, 1943, 1944) each play in under two hours, making the game accessible to tournament players and busy enthusiasts alike.

Designer: Mark Herman.
